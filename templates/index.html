About the System

This web application uses Machine Learning algorithms to analyze iris flower parameters such as sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width. It predicts the species of the iris flower (Setosa, Versicolor, or Virginica). It supports accurate classification and assists researchers and students in understanding plant species identification.
